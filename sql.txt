You're now going to setup SQL, it's very easy to do and little effort.

Instructions:
1. Use the command:
sudo mysql_secure_installation

Then type your password in for root
Press enter again
Type 'n' and then keep pressing enter once it says "Thanks for using MariaDB"

2. Go to http://localhost/phpmyadmin/ and login
Username: root
You will use your computer's password for the password.

Go to "Databases" find in the top menu bar
Then the box where it says "Database name", you're going to put "wordpress".
Then click create.

3. Once you checkmark the box below and continue, this script
will update a few more things to prepare Wordpress and you 
should be on your way.
